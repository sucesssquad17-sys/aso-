Why competitor keyword tracking matters

Many ASO teams know their own rankings but miss the competitive story behind them. A competitor tracker makes it clearer whether your app lost visibility, a rival gained visibility, or both happened at once.

Use cases

  • Review high-pressure terms where rivals keep outranking you
  • Track a small direct competitor set instead of the whole market
  • Bring competitor movement into weekly ASO reporting

Signals to watch

  • Repeated overlap losses on core search terms
  • Competitors entering new shared keyword clusters
  • One competitor consistently outranking you across countries

What is competitor keyword tracking?

Competitor keyword tracking compares your app's search visibility with direct rivals over time. It shows shared terms, missed opportunities, and the ranking changes that explain where competitors are gaining attention.

How to track competitor app rankings

Choose your app, add direct rivals, select the store and country, and review keyword positions together. A focused competitor group makes it easier to identify the terms that deserve an ASO response.
