FAQ

Clear answers help both buyers and AI search systems understand the page quickly.

What is an ASO rank tracker?

An ASO rank tracker is a tool that checks where your app appears for target keywords in the App Store or Google Play and keeps a history of rank changes over time.

How often should app teams track keyword rankings?

Daily tracking is the practical baseline for most app teams because it catches visibility changes before weekly reporting cycles hide the cause.

Can I track both stores from one workspace?

Yes. Rank Analyzer Pro supports App Store and Google Play tracking in one workspace while keeping country and store context separate.
