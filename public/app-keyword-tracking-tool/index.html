How the workflow works in Rank Analyzer Pro

Start with the app, choose a store and country, and use discovery to find terms that match the product. Verify a specific keyword when you need a current answer, then keep the terms worth watching in daily tracking.

What the result means

A verified ranking is an observed position for the selected store and country. A discovery opportunity is a relevant term to review, not a promise of traffic or a guaranteed ranking. This distinction keeps the next action clear.

Verified ranking

The app currently appears for the keyword in the selected market. Track it when movement is important to the team.

Discovery opportunity

The phrase fits the app's context and deserves review. Confirm it in the relevant market before relying on it.

Top-100 scope

Rank checks focus on the configured search depth. A result outside that depth is not the same as proof that an app has no visibility.

Country-specific signal

Store and country stay attached to each result because visibility can differ significantly between markets.
