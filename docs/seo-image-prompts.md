# SEO Editorial Image Prompts

These prompts produced the original conceptual illustrations used on the SEO landing pages and keyword-discovery guide. They deliberately avoid product UI, store logos, competitor brands, customer data, and claims of measured performance.

## Shared direction

Use a polished editorial vector illustration for a modern app analytics publication. Pale ice-blue background, deep navy structure, electric blue, cyan, teal, and restrained amber accents. Soft depth, subtle grain, crisp geometric forms, generous negative space, no readable text, no numbers, no logos, no screenshots, no browser chrome, no realistic app interface, no Apple or Google symbols, no brand marks, and no purple-dominant palette.
